Our Core Values

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

Rooted in the spiritual legacy of Sheikh Fareed Auliya, we envision a transformative learning environment where faith enlightens intellect and knowledge shapes character. Our vision is to cultivate spiritually grounded, intellectually empowered individuals who lead with wisdom, compassion, and moral clarity, contributing positively to a just and enlightened society.

Our Mission

Guided by the values of Sheikh Fareed Auliya and operated by the Odamala Mahallu Committee, Sheikh Fareed Auliya Da'wa College is committed to integrating traditional Islamic scholarship with contemporary education through a structured ten-year academic journey. Our mission is to nurture holistic growth—spiritual, intellectual, and ethical—preparing students to serve humanity through education, Da'wa, leadership, and social responsibility.

Our Journey

Sheikh Fareed Auliya Dawa College is an affiliated institution of Darul Huda Islamic University. Located at Odamala near Perinthalmanna in the Malappuram district, the institution was established on October 14, 2009. It is proudly run by the Odamala Mahallu committee and operates under the spiritual nurturing of the eminent Sufi saint Sheikh Fareed Auliya.
